ICH Computational Biology Club

Based at UCL’s Great Ormond Street Institute of Child Health, the ICH Computational Biology Club meets once a month to discuss recent computational topics and papers, often presented by their authors.

It is co-organised by Yara Sanchez Corrales and George Hall, two postdoctoral research fellows in the lab of Prof Sergi Castellano. If you are interested in speaking, please get in touch! Our club hosts both internal and external speakers.
